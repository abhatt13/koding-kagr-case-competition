## The Challenge
Following the NCAA vs. House settlement, Midwest State University must generate **$20.5M annually** to compensate student-athletes while maintaining competitive athletic programs.

## Our Solution
A comprehensive **7-initiative revenue strategy** projected to generate **$25.5M** in incremental annual revenue, exceeding the target by **124.5%**.

---

## Key Findings from Data Analysis

### Current State (312 Events Analyzed)
- **Total Annual Revenue:** $94.36M
- **Largest Revenue Stream:** Ticket sales (42.2%)
- **Top Sport:** Football ($38M, 86.7% capacity)
- **Biggest Opportunity:** Women's Basketball (43.5% capacity - 56.5% of seats empty)

### Customer Insights (95 Survey Responses)
- **High Satisfaction:** 8.37/10 overall rating
- **Strong Advocacy:** 70% would recommend (NPS leaders)
- **Demographics:** 63% aged 18-25, balanced gender split
- **Underserved Segment:** Corporate clients (only 9.2% of attendance)

### Critical Gaps Identified
1. **No dynamic pricing** - missing $4.8M opportunity
2. **Women's sports undermarketed** - $4.4M potential
3. **Corporate underdevelopment** - $7.5M opportunity
4. **Low football merchandise** - $1.5M gap
5. **Limited digital engagement** - $5M potential

---

## Strategic Recommendations

### Initiative 1: Dynamic Pricing Implementation
- **Revenue:** $4.8M annually
- **Tactic:** AI-driven pricing adjusting by demand, opponent, game importance
- **Proven Success:** University of Texas increased ticket revenue 12%
- **Timeline:** Q1 2026 launch

### Initiative 2: Women's Basketball Growth Campaign
- **Revenue:** $4.4M annually
- **Tactic:** Themed promotions, family packages, enhanced experience
- **Target:** Increase capacity utilization from 43.5% to 60%
- **Timeline:** Q2 2026 season launch

### Initiative 3: Corporate Partnership Expansion
- **Revenue:** $7.5M annually
- **Tactic:** Dedicated sales team, tiered packages ($10K-$50K)
- **Proven Success:** Peer institutions generating $1.3M+ in year 1
- **Timeline:** Q1 2026 team hire and launch

### Initiative 4: Football Merchandise Optimization
- **Revenue:** $1.5M annually
- **Tactic:** Mobile pop-ups, limited editions, NIL player merchandise
- **Target:** Increase merchandise penetration from 9% to 13%
- **Timeline:** Q1 2026 rollout

### Initiative 5: Digital Fan Engagement
- **Revenue:** $5.0M annually
- **Tactic:** Content strategy targeting Gen Z, influencer partnerships
- **Research Basis:** Each follower generates $496 in revenue
- **Target:** +10,000 social media followers
- **Timeline:** Ongoing from Q1 2026

### Initiative 6: Premium Seating Expansion
- **Revenue:** $600K annually
- **Tactic:** Add 100 club seats with enhanced amenities
- **Price Point:** $120 vs $56 average
- **Timeline:** Q3 2026 completion

### Initiative 7: Off-Peak Game Promotions
- **Revenue:** $1.8M annually
- **Tactic:** Student rush, group sales, 2-for-1 promotions
- **Target:** 30% attendance boost for bottom quartile games
- **Timeline:** Q2 2026 launch
