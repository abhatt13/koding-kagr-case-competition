## SLIDE 6: Industry Best Practices

### Dynamic Pricing Success Stories
- **University of Texas:** 12% ticket revenue increase with AI-driven pricing
- **UC Berkeley, Georgetown, Purdue:** Successfully implemented dynamic pricing
- **Best Practice:** $3-5 increments, max 5 daily adjustments, protect season ticket holders

### Social Media ROI
- **$496 revenue per social media follower** (industry research)
- **90% of Gen Z** consume sports content via social media
- **2.33% Instagram engagement** for sports vs. 1.22% industry average

### Corporate Partnerships
- Schools with outbound sales teams: **$1.3M+ additional first-year revenue**
- Premium experiences driving 20-30% of high-revenue events

## SLIDE 7: Strategic Recommendations

### Initiative 1: Dynamic Pricing Implementation
**Timeline:** Q1 2026
**Projected Revenue:** $4,779,216

**Action Steps:**
- Partner with pricing technology platform (Digonex, Paciolan)
- Implement variable pricing based on opponent type, game importance, day of week
- Protect season ticket holders with lowest-price guarantees
- Start with Men's Basketball and Football (high-demand sports)

**Success Metrics:**
- 12% ticket revenue increase
- Improved capacity utilization for mid-tier games
- Higher revenue per seat for rivalry/tournament games

3. **Women's Basketball Marketing Doesn't Drive Attendance**
   *Mitigation:* A/B test promotions, adjust tactics based on data, leverage successful men's basketball playbook
