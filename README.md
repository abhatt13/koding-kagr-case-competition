## Executive Summary

This repository contains comprehensive data analysis and strategic recommendations for Midwest State University's Athletic Department to generate $20.5M in incremental revenue to offset NCAA vs. House settlement costs.

Through detailed analysis of 312 athletic events and 95 customer survey responses, we've identified **7 strategic initiatives projected to generate $25.5M annually** (124.5% of target).

## Key Findings

### Revenue Analysis
- **Current Total Revenue:** $94.36M
- **Revenue Breakdown:**
  - Ticket Revenue: 42.2%
  - Concession Revenue: 30.5%
  - Parking Revenue: 14.8%
  - Merchandise Revenue: 12.5%

### Critical Opportunities Identified

1. **Women's Basketball Capacity Optimization**
   - Current capacity utilization: 43.5%
   - Revenue per attendee: $49.48 (nearly matches Men's Basketball)
   - **Opportunity:** Increase attendance to 60% capacity
   - **Projected Impact:** $4.4M annually

2. **Corporate Partnership Expansion**
   - Current corporate attendance: 9.2%
   - Industry benchmark: 15%
   - **Opportunity:** Dedicated corporate sales team and tiered packages
   - **Projected Impact:** $7.5M annually

3. **Dynamic Pricing Implementation**
   - Apply surge pricing for high-demand games
   - Off-peak discounts to fill capacity
   - **Projected Impact:** $3.8M annually

4. **Premium Experience Enhancement**
   - Club seats, VIP lounges, improved concessions
   - **Projected Impact:** $4.2M annually

5. **Digital Transformation**
   - Mobile app, streaming, in-app purchases
   - **Projected Impact:** $2.8M annually

6. **Merchandise & Licensing Expansion**
   - Pop-up stores, online marketplace, NIL merchandise
   - **Projected Impact:** $1.9M annually

7. **Alumni Engagement Program**
   - Lifetime passes, exclusive events, tailgate packages
   - **Projected Impact:** $0.9M annually

**Total Projected Revenue:** $25.5M (124.5% of $20.5M target)
